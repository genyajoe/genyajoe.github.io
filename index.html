Genya is a Ph.D. Candidate at the Department of Computer Science, The University of Texas at Dallas. He is interested in 5G network slicing, interdependency of Virtual Network Functions (VNFs), learning algorithms such as Deep Reinforcement Learning and Online Convex Optimization. He received B.S. and M.S. degrees in Engineering from Soka University, Japan. He has been awarded two Ph.D. Fellowships from JASSO (funded by the Japanese government) and Shigeta Education Foundation. His public CV is available here.
