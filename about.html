Summary

Genya is a second-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Texas at Dallas. His research interest lies in graph optimization problems for design of layered networks and Software Defined Networking (SDN). Currently, He is working on survivability problems in interdependent networks in Advanced Networks Research Lab led by Professor Jason P. Jue.

Genya received Master and Bachelor of Science in Engineering from Soka University, Tokyo Japan.
